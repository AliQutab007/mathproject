  3. A company is providing internet plans for a short period. If company charges 10 dollars for an hour. What are the domain and range of this function?


    Step 1 :  

    Since company is charging 10 dollars for an hour.


    1 hour(h) = 10 dollars(d)



    Step 2 :  

    Domain = All positive real numbers (h $\geq$ 0)


    Because user can’t use internet plans for negative hours


    Range = All positive real numbers (d $\geq$ 0)


    Because company can’t make negative money


    Note: Company can make 0 dollars if no user takes their plans.



    Answer  :  

    Domain = All positive real numbers

     

    Range = All positive real numbers



  4. Jacsina is selling her accessories to raise funds for poor children. The cost of each accessory is 1 dollar. Find the domain and range? 


    Step 1 :  

    : Let she made x amount by selling c number of her accessories.


    x(c) = 1 . c



    Step 2 :  

    Domain = Whole numbers (except 0, because she cant sell zero accessories).


    Range = Whole numbers (She makes zero dollar if she sells zero her accessories).



    Answer  :  

    Domain = Whole numbers (except 0)

    Range = Whole numbers



  5. In one of the college admission tests, Smith has simplified the expression $\frac{x+1}{x^2+10x+9}$. Later he tried to find the domain for this expression. What will be the answer.


    Step 1 :  

    Given expression is $\frac{x+1}{x^2+10x+9}$



    Step 2 :  

    Find the factors of denominator x$^2$+10x+9


    x$^2$+10x+9 = x$^2$+9x+x+9 = x(x + 9) + (x + 9) = (x + 1)(x + 9)


    Factors of denominators are (x + 1)(x + 9)


    Domain = All real numbers except -9 or -1.



    Answer  :  

     All real numbers except -9 or -1.
